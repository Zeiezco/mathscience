Developer Student Clubs

DSC which stands for Developer Student Clubs are university based community groups for
interested students interested in Google developer technologies.
Student from all undergraduate or graduate programs with
an interest in growing as a developer are welcome.
By joining a DSC, student's grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment
and build solutions for local businesses and their community.
